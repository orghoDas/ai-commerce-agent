# Product Brief

## Product

An AI sales and support agent for small and medium businesses. It answers customer product questions, checks stock and price, creates orders, and produces daily admin reports.

## Target Customers

Start with one niche. Good early options:

- Fashion and clothing shops
- Cosmetics stores
- Phone and accessory shops
- Small electronics sellers
- Restaurants with fixed menus
- Spare parts sellers

Avoid building for every business type at launch. The product becomes easier to sell when your demo, wording, dashboard, and edge cases match one industry.

## Main Users

Customer:

- Asks about product availability
- Asks price
- Sends product name, description, or image
- Places an order
- Checks order status

Business admin:

- Manages products, prices, and stock
- Reviews orders
- Takes over conversations
- Receives daily stock/order report
- Configures agent behavior and business policies

## Core Promise

The business gets a 24/7 sales assistant that reduces repeated customer messages while keeping inventory and orders accurate.

## Non-Negotiables

- The agent must not invent stock.
- The agent must not invent prices.
- The agent must not confirm an order without inventory reservation.
- The admin must be able to audit what happened.
- The customer must be able to reach a human when needed.
